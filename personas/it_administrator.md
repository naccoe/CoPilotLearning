# IT/Systems Administrator - Persona

## 👤 Profile

**Name:** Daniel Park  
**Role:** IT/Systems Administrator  
**Experience:** 8 years in healthcare IT  
**Department:** Information Technology  
**Reports To:** Office Manager  
**Certifications:** CompTIA A+, Network+, Healthcare IT

## 📋 Responsibilities

### Primary Duties
- Maintain EMR/EHR system (Electronic Medical Records)
- Network and server infrastructure management
- User account management and access control
- System backups and disaster recovery
- Help desk support for 120 users
- Security and HIPAA compliance
- Software updates and patch management
- Hardware procurement and maintenance

### Secondary Duties
- Vendor relationship management
- IT budget planning
- Technology training for staff
- Evaluate new technology solutions
- Document IT procedures
- Cybersecurity monitoring

## 😫 Daily Pain Points

### 1. Help Desk Overwhelm
**Problem:** Solo IT support for 120 users across all systems  
**Impact:**
- 30-50 help tickets daily
- Average response time 4+ hours
- Critical issues compete with password resets
- Staff frustration and lost productivity
- No time for proactive maintenance
- After-hours emergency calls

**Current Workaround:** Triage by who yells loudest, many issues unresolved

### 2. EMR System Performance Issues
**Problem:** Aging EMR system is slow and crashes frequently  
**Impact:**
- 2-3 system crashes weekly during clinic hours
- Providers can't access patient records
- 15-20 minute downtime per crash
- Lost clinical productivity ($1K+ per incident)
- Patient safety concerns
- Staff constantly complaining

**Current Workaround:** Restart servers, apologize, "it's working now"

### 3. Data Integration Nightmare
**Problem:** 8+ disconnected systems don't talk to each other  
**Impact:**
- Manual data entry between systems
- Data inconsistencies and errors
- No single source of truth
- Reports require manual compilation
- Duplicate patient records
- Staff waste time on data reconciliation

**Current Workaround:** Export/import CSV files, pray for no errors

### 4. Security and Compliance Anxiety
**Problem:** Limited tools for HIPAA compliance monitoring  
**Impact:**
- Can't easily audit who accessed what patient data
- No automated security alerts
- Compliance violations risk ($50K+ fines)
- Sleep poorly worrying about breaches
- Manual log reviews (time-consuming)
- Outdated access controls

**Current Workaround:** Quarterly manual audits, fingers crossed

### 5. Backup and Disaster Recovery Gaps
**Problem:** Backup system is manual and unreliable  
**Impact:**
- Last disaster recovery test: 18 months ago
- Not confident backups would actually work
- No RTO/RPO monitoring
- Critical data at risk
- Would take days to recover from major failure
- Compliance risk

**Current Workaround:** Weekly manual backup checks, hope for the best

### 6. Reporting and Analytics Limitations
**Problem:** Can't easily extract meaningful data from EMR  
**Impact:**
- Leadership constantly asking for reports IT can't provide
- Hours spent on custom SQL queries
- No self-service reporting for managers
- Can't prove ROI of technology investments
- Miss opportunities for improvement

**Current Workaround:** Manually export data, manipulate in Excel
