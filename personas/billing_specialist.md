# Billing Specialist - Persona

## 👤 Profile

**Name:** Patricia "Pat" O'Brien  
**Role:** Senior Billing Specialist  
**Experience:** 10 years in medical billing  
**Department:** Revenue Cycle Management  
**Reports To:** Billing Manager  
**Certification:** Certified Professional Biller (CPB)

## 📋 Responsibilities

### Primary Duties
- Process and submit insurance claims (40-60 daily)
- Post insurance payments and adjustments
- Follow up on denied and pending claims
- Generate patient statements
- Handle billing inquiries from patients
- Verify procedure and diagnosis codes
- Reconcile daily batch reports
- Process refunds and payment plans

### Secondary Duties
- Train new billing staff
- Monthly aging report review
- Coordinate with clinical staff on coding questions
- Update fee schedules and payer contracts
- Audit claims before submission

## 😫 Daily Pain Points

### 1. High Claim Rejection Rate
**Problem:** 18% of claims rejected on first submission  
**Impact:**
- Rework takes 15-20 minutes per claim
- Delayed payment (30-60 days extra)
- $95K monthly in delayed revenue
- Staff frustration and overtime

**Current Workaround:** Manual review and resubmission, but can't catch everything upfront

### 2. Coding Errors from Providers
**Problem:** Missing or incorrect ICD-10/CPT codes  
**Impact:**
- Claims rejected for invalid code combinations
- Must request clarification from busy providers
- Delays in claim submission (3-5 days)
- Under-coding loses revenue

**Current Workaround:** Send queries to providers, often takes days to get response

### 3. Insurance Verification Gaps
**Problem:** Patients seen without proper verification  
**Impact:**
- Claims denied for inactive coverage
- Patients billed who thought they had coverage
- Collections issues
- Provider frustration

**Current Workaround:** Retroactive verification, negotiate with patients

### 4. Denial Management Backlog
**Problem:** 200+ denied claims needing follow-up  
**Impact:**
- Claims age past timely filing limits
- Write-offs increase ($8K-12K monthly)
- Overwhelming workload
- Missed revenue

**Current Workaround:** Prioritize by dollar amount, smaller claims often written off

### 5. Patient Payment Collection
**Problem:** Patient responsibility increasing, but collection rate low  
**Impact:**
- $180K in outstanding patient balances
- Time-consuming collections calls
- Bad debt write-offs
- Staff avoids collections (unpleasant)

**Current Workaround:** Send statements, occasional calls, eventually write off

### 6. Payer Policy Changes
**Problem:** 25+ insurance companies with frequent policy updates  
**Impact:**
- Claims denied for new requirements
- No systematic tracking of policy changes
- Constant research needed
- Compliance risks

**Current Workaround:** Subscribe to payer bulletins, but overwhelming to track
